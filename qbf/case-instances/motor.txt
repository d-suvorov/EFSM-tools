## To test the program, run the simulator, and quickly simulate the press and release of button

((!y0 && y2) UNTIL x1) || ALWAYS (!x1)

ALWAYS (x2 -> (((!y0 && y2) UNTIL x1) || ALWAYS (!x1)))
